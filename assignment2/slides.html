1.Introduction

The Axios-Harris Poll 100 is a trusted raking of the reputation of the companies most on the minds of Americans using a framework Harris has used since 1999.http://theharrispoll.com/partners/media/axios-harrispoll-100/

2.Dataset

Title: 2022 Corporate Reputation and poll Rankings

Year: 2022

Source: Thomas Mock (2022). Tidy Tuesday: A weekly data project aimed at the R ecosystem. https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday.

Purpose of Dataset: Find the top 100 companies Reputation Quotient

Content: Reputation:company,industry,name,score,rank; Poll: company industry 2022_rank 2022_rq change year rank rq

Summary: This data set describes attributes of the Reputation and poll of Companies so the data set can be make used to propose exploratory data analysis.

3.Organization

Weve decided to find the average score of all reputation attributes and the reputation quotient (RQ) for each company and add them both into one data set for cleaner and easier exploration.

Reputation Attributes Score -> Average Reputation

Reputation Quotient -> Average Reputation Quotient

Merge Both Averages
